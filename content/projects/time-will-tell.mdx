## Overview

*Time Will Tell* is an immersive audiovisual projection experiment inspired by Hikaru Utada’s music and visual world. The project combines stop-motion aesthetics, AI-generated imagery, and spatial projection to explore how sound and moving image can shape emotional perception within a physical environment.

<div className="zh">
《Time Will Tell》是一项受宇多田光音乐与视觉世界启发的沉浸式视听投影实验。项目结合定格动画美学、AI 生成影像与空间投影，探索声音与动态视觉如何在物理空间中塑造情绪感知。
</div>

## Process

![Stop-motion process](/images/projects/project-01-time-will-tell-vj-project/01-process.jpg)

The visual system began with small stop-motion scenes, using character-based gestures and soft transitions to create an intimate rhythm. These early frames established the emotional tone of the project before the visuals moved into an AI-assisted workflow.

<div className="zh">
视觉系统最初从定格动画场景开始，通过角色动作与柔和转场建立一种亲密的节奏感。这些早期画面奠定了项目的情绪基调，也成为后续 AI 辅助影像生成的基础。
</div>

![Runway process](/images/projects/project-01-time-will-tell-vj-project/02-process.jpg)

AI tools were used to extend the hand-made visual language into moving image sequences. Through Runway and image-generation workflows, I tested how illustrated scenes could become animated fragments while still keeping a soft, handmade atmosphere.

<div className="zh">
项目使用 AI 工具将手工绘制的视觉语言延展为动态影像序列。通过 Runway 与图像生成工作流，我测试插画场景如何被转化为动态片段，同时保留柔和、手作感的视觉氛围。
</div>

![Projection control console](/images/projects/project-01-time-will-tell-vj-project/03-process.jpg)

The final visuals were arranged and tested for spatial playback using TouchDesigner and MadMapper. In the Audio Lab, the projection setup allowed the visuals to move beyond a single screen and become part of the surrounding environment.

<div className="zh">
最终画面通过 TouchDesigner 与 MadMapper 进行空间播放与投影测试。在 Audio Lab 中，投影设置让影像不再只是单一屏幕上的内容，而是成为包围空间的一部分。
</div>

## Outcome

![Final projection photo](/images/projects/project-01-time-will-tell-vj-project/04-final.jpg)

The final installation focused on atmosphere rather than plot. Through projection scale, sound, rhythm, and character-based animation, the room became a quiet and intimate viewing environment.

<div className="zh">
最终作品更关注氛围的建立，而不是完整情节的讲述。通过投影尺度、声音节奏与角色动画，整个房间被转化为一个安静、亲密的观看环境。
</div>

![Final visual still](/images/projects/project-01-time-will-tell-vj-project/05-final.jpg)

This project became an early experiment in combining AI-generated visuals with immersive spatial media, and it shaped my later interest in projection, audiovisual systems, and emotionally responsive environments.

<div className="zh">
这个项目是我早期将 AI 生成影像与沉浸式空间媒体结合的实验，也影响了我后来对投影、视听系统与情绪化空间体验的持续兴趣。
</div>
