## Overview

*Interactive Dollhouse* is a physical computing and projection mapping installation created with Yutong at NYU ITP. The project turns a small dollhouse into a responsive inner world, where a sheep character becomes the key to activating different emotional and spatial moments.

<div className="zh">
《Interactive Dollhouse》是我与 Yutong 在 NYU ITP 合作完成的物理计算与投影映射装置。项目将一个小型娃娃屋转化为一个会回应观众动作的内在世界，其中小羊角色成为触发不同情绪与空间场景的关键。
</div>

Inspired by the gentle atmosphere of *Animal Crossing* and childhood memories of pretend play, the project uses softness, cuteness, and nostalgia as emotional entry points. Instead of creating a complex control system, we designed the interaction around one simple gesture: placing the sheep inside the house.

<div className="zh">
项目受到《Animal Crossing》的温柔氛围和童年过家家记忆的启发，以柔软、可爱和怀旧作为情感入口。我们没有设计复杂的控制方式，而是将互动集中在一个非常简单的动作上：把小羊放进娃娃屋的不同位置。
</div>

When the sheep is placed in different zones, the dollhouse responds through projection, light, and sound. This makes the miniature space feel alive, as if each room has its own mood and memory.

<div className="zh">
当小羊被放置在不同区域时，娃娃屋会通过投影、灯光和声音作出回应。这个微缩空间因此变得像是有生命一样，每个房间都拥有自己的情绪和记忆。
</div>

## Process

![Concept and interaction sketches](/images/projects/project-04-interactive-dollhouse/01-process.png)

The project began with the idea of treating the dollhouse as an inner emotional world. We first mapped out how the sheep could move through different zones, and how each placement could trigger a different spatial response.

<div className="zh">
项目最初的概念是将娃娃屋视为一个内在的情绪世界。我们先梳理小羊如何在不同区域之间移动，以及每一次放置如何触发不同的空间反馈。
</div>

![System architecture and Arduino testing](/images/projects/project-04-interactive-dollhouse/03-process.jpg)

Arduino UNO acted as the nervous system of the installation. It read sensor input from different areas of the dollhouse and coordinated the real-time response between projection, light, and sound. A small physical movement was translated into a layered sensory experience.

<div className="zh">
Arduino UNO 在装置中像是整个系统的神经中枢。它读取娃娃屋不同区域的传感器输入，并实时协调投影、灯光与声音之间的反馈。一个很小的实体动作因此被转化为多层次的感官体验。
</div>

![Dollhouse design and projection surface](/images/projects/project-04-interactive-dollhouse/04-process.jpg)

The dollhouse was designed as a small-scale and intimate structure. Every wall, floor, and object became a potential projection surface. Through projection mapping, the rooms were transformed into narrative zones where light could define mood, atmosphere, and emotional tone.

<div className="zh">
娃娃屋被设计成一个小尺度、亲密的空间结构。每一面墙、地板和物件都可以成为潜在的投影表面。通过投影映射，不同房间被转化为叙事区域，光线也参与塑造空间的情绪、氛围和情感基调。
</div>

![Development process](/images/projects/project-04-interactive-dollhouse/05-process.jpg)

The development process moved through sketching, prototyping, mapping, and integration. We first planned the interaction zones, then tested the reed switches and outputs with Arduino. After that, we calibrated the projection surfaces and assembled the wiring, sound, and media system into one working installation.

<div className="zh">
项目开发过程经历了草图规划、原型测试、投影校准和系统整合几个阶段。我们先规划互动区域，再用 Arduino 测试磁簧开关和输出效果，之后校准投影表面，并将线路、声音和媒体系统整合成一个可运行的装置。
</div>

## Outcome

![Final interactive experience](/images/projects/project-04-interactive-dollhouse/07-final.jpg)

The final installation became a responsive miniature environment. When users moved the sheep to different locations, the dollhouse reacted with projected animations, colored light, and sound. The result was a small living space where physical interaction and digital media worked together.

<div className="zh">
最终装置形成了一个会回应用户动作的微缩环境。当用户将小羊移动到不同位置时，娃娃屋会通过投影动画、彩色灯光和声音作出反应。整个作品变成了一个由实体互动与数字媒体共同构成的小型生活空间。
</div>

Rather than designing an interface on a screen, this project explored how interaction can live inside a physical space. The dollhouse became more than an object; it became a reactive environment that changed with the viewer’s presence, touch, and movement.

<div className="zh">
这个项目并不是在屏幕上设计一个界面，而是探索互动如何存在于真实的物理空间中。娃娃屋不再只是一个物件，而是一个会随着观众的出现、触碰和移动而变化的反应式环境。
</div>

Through this project, I learned how physical computing, projection mapping, and sound can work together as one spatial interface. It also expanded my understanding of interaction design: an interface can be a room, an object, a gesture, an atmosphere, or an emotional response.

<div className="zh">
通过这个项目，我学习到物理计算、投影映射和声音系统如何共同构成一种空间化的界面。它也拓展了我对交互设计的理解：界面不只可以是屏幕和按钮，也可以是房间、物件、动作、氛围和情绪回应。
</div>
