*Pearl Fantasy* is an AIGC experimental video built around a surreal encounter between a rabbit and a shell that produces pearls. The project explores a soft, dreamlike visual language through AI-generated images, animated fragments, and simple narrative editing.

<div className="zh">
《Pearl Fantasy》是一个围绕兔子与产出珍珠的蚌展开的 AIGC 实验影像。项目通过 AI 生成图像、动态片段和简单叙事剪辑，探索一种柔软、梦幻且带有童话感的视觉语言。
</div>

The images were first generated with Midjourney, then animated and extended through Runway. I edited the final sequence in Jianying, combining different fragments into a short visual experiment about fantasy, transformation, and fragile beauty.

<div className="zh">
画面首先通过 Midjourney 生成，再使用 Runway 进行动态化与延展。最终影像在剪映中完成剪辑，将不同片段组合成一个关于幻想、转化与脆弱美感的短片实验。
</div>
