*Travel Journals* is a collection of photographs taken during different trips. Instead of focusing on landmarks, these images document small scenes, textures, streets, plants, architecture, and quiet moments that shaped my memory of each place.

<div className="zh">
《旅游日记》是一组在不同旅行途中拍摄的照片。相比标志性景点，这些影像更关注街道、植物、建筑、材质和安静的日常片段，记录我对一个地方的感受和记忆。
</div>
