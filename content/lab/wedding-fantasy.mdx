*Wedding Fantasy* is an experimental film about my parents’ marriage and the emotional impact of their arguments. By combining old wedding footage, family videos, and wedding blessing audio from the 2000s, the work reflects on how love, memory, and domestic conflict can exist within the same archive.

<div className="zh">
《Wedding Fantasy》是一部关于我父母婚姻关系与家庭争吵影响的实验影像。作品结合旧婚礼录像、家庭影像和 2000 年代的婚礼祝福声音，思考爱、记忆与家庭冲突如何同时存在于同一份私人档案中。
</div>

Alongside the video, I created a small sculpture using Polaroid emulsion lift: an image of a hand with a wedding ring transferred onto an apple, pierced with pins. The object appears over the wedding footage like a warning, symbolizing the wounds hidden inside marriage.

<div className="zh">
除了影像之外，我还制作了一个小型雕塑：使用 Polaroid emulsion lift 将戴着婚戒的手转印到苹果上，并用针刺穿。这个物件在婚礼影像上方旋转，像一个警示，也象征婚姻中隐藏的伤口。
</div>
