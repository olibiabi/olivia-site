*Exhibition Notes* is a collection of small drawings and visual notes made during or after exhibition visits. Rather than documenting artworks exactly, these sketches capture fragments that stayed with me: gestures, colors, compositions, moods, and small details.

<div className="zh">
《看展随笔》是一组在看展过程中或看展之后完成的速写与视觉笔记。它们并不是对作品的准确记录，而是捕捉那些留在我脑海里的片段：人物姿态、色彩、构图、情绪和细节。
</div>
