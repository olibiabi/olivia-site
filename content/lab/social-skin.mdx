*Social Skin* is an AIGC speculative video about a future where everyone communicates through avatars, but digital appearance becomes a new form of social class. The project reflects on how virtual identity, beauty standards, and platform systems may reproduce real-world inequality.

<div className="zh">
《Social Skin》是一个关于未来虚拟身份的 AIGC 推想影像。在这个设定中，人们通过 avatar 进行交流，但数字外貌逐渐成为新的社会阶级标志。作品思考虚拟身份、审美标准与平台系统如何重新复制现实中的不平等。
</div>

The story follows a mid-level interviewer meeting applicants with low-tier default avatars, until a superior with a luxurious avatar appears and orders them to keep working. Through this fictional workplace scene, the video questions what counts as “yourself” when identity is increasingly shaped by digital surfaces.

<div className="zh">
故事围绕一位中层面试官展开：他面试着使用低等级默认 avatar 的求职者，直到一位拥有高级 avatar 的上司出现，并命令他继续工作。通过这个虚构的职场场景，影像追问当身份越来越被数字表面塑造时，什么才是真正的“自己”。
</div>
