*Hotdog* is an absurd AIGC short film created for the GenArt NYU AI Film Hackathon. Developed with @olibiabi, the project turns a simple and ridiculous idea into a short narrative experiment where absurdity becomes the story itself.

<div className="zh">
《Hotdog》是一个为 GenArt NYU AI Film Hackathon 创作的荒诞 AIGC 短片。项目与 @olibiabi 合作完成，将一个简单又离奇的想法发展成一个短小的叙事实验，让荒诞本身成为故事。
</div>

The video was built through an iterative AI workflow across NanoBanana, Kling, and Runway, moving from image generation to motion testing and final timing. It was selected as the funniest film of the hackathon.

<div className="zh">
影像通过 NanoBanana、Kling 和 Runway 之间的迭代工作流完成，从图像生成、动态测试到最终节奏剪辑逐步成型。这个作品最终获得了 hackathon 的 funniest film。
</div>
