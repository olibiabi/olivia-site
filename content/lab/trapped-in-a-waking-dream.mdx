*Trapped in a Waking Dream* is an experimental film about the silence that follows family conflict. After witnessing a severe argument between my parents, I found myself caught between the fear of another outburst and the strange calm of everyone pretending nothing had happened.

<div className="zh">
《Trapped in a Waking Dream》是一部关于家庭冲突之后的沉默的实验影像。在目睹父母一次激烈争吵后，我被困在对下一次爆发的恐惧，以及所有人假装什么都没有发生的平静之中。
</div>

The film combines old footage recorded by my father with my own clips from a vintage camera. These happy childhood images feel distant and almost unreal, while the narration, written as a poem, reflects my sorrow, helplessness, and desire to escape into memory.

<div className="zh">
影片结合了父亲过去拍摄的家庭录像和我用复古相机拍摄的片段。那些快乐的童年画面显得遥远而不真实，而我自己写下的诗性旁白，则记录了悲伤、无力感，以及想要逃回记忆中的愿望。
</div>
