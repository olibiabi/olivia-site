*Graphic Design* is a collection of visual experiments exploring patterns, symbols, typography, and poster composition. These works focus on playful graphic systems, repeated forms, and the relationship between color, structure, and visual rhythm.

<div className="zh">
《Graphic Design》是一组平面视觉实验，探索图案、符号、字体与海报构成。这些作品关注具有玩味感的视觉系统、重复形态，以及色彩、结构和视觉节奏之间的关系。
</div>
