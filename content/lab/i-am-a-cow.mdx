*I Am A Cow* is a hand-drawn experimental animation about academic pressure, emotional distress, and an unhealthy cycle of binge eating and purging. At the beginning of my third year in university, I felt overwhelmed by expectations around graduation and postgraduate applications.

<div className="zh">
《I Am A Cow》是一部手绘实验动画，关于学业压力、情绪困境，以及暴食与催吐之间不健康的循环。大三开始时，我被毕业和研究生申请的期待压得喘不过气。
</div>

Each time food came back from my stomach, I felt like a cow ruminating. Instead of using realistic imagery, I chose childlike doodles and a simple hand-drawn style to express a painful experience in a softer, more indirect way.

<div className="zh">
每一次食物从胃里涌回来时，我都觉得自己像一头反刍的牛。我没有使用写实的影像，而是选择用儿童涂鸦般的手绘风格，以更柔软、间接的方式表达这段痛苦经历。
</div>
